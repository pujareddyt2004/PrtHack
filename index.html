FIGHTING CLIMATE CHANGE ONE HOME AT A TIME

"Climate Change"


Two ominous words that seem to have incited fear and panic everywhere we go. These words show up while we are driving our cars, lighting our fireplaces, constantly looming over us with the threat of global destruction.


Only as a society together, we can make an impact on climate change.


Changing our paths towards an eco-friendly home is a great way to accomplish this goal. In the US alone, household heating and cooling systems account for 11% of our overall carbon emissions. Whether it be finding a new home, renovating, or making smaller lifestyle changes, eco-friendly homes are a great way to start.

Let your home be in your control

Our platform aims to give individuals a chance moniter their energy usage within their homes, as well as identifying different energy sources. This platform analyzes data from your home and shows individuals what they can improve on from a dashboard. It's simple. It's easy. And it's fast.
